Looping Crystal

Sinopsis: Looping Crystal is a 2D platform game where you have to help Nea escape from a mysterious cave while learning different skills to avoid being cooked by lava. You'd better help her, I don't think Nea knows how to swim in lava.

Released: 2021

Studio: Virtual Ravens Games

Plataform: PC

Skill Focus: Production & Game Design

Time Spent: 1 week

Engine and Tools: Unity, Trello, Miro

Contributions

Producer

The real challenge of being in charge of the production of this project was not coordinating the team, as we were a small group. The greatest difficulty was having to manage all the tasks in a short period of time.

Despite this, we were able to manage all the tasks necessary to complete the project satisfactorily.

Game Designer

My work within the design department was varied.

First, I was in charge of designing the different mechanics and how to present them to the player. In this game, the player is trapped in a loop every time they die and can unlock new skills each time they encounter a new obstacle. So my task was to teach the player the new mechanics they could use to overcome the different obstacles they encounter.

I was also in charge of level design, responsible for designing the path our protagonist had to take. This design was done using tiles in Unity, and a progressive level design was created as the different mechanics were added.
