Origins

Sinopsis: Origins is a local multiplayer game where you have to help your space cats recover dark matter. Be careful, because each cat will want to keep it for as long as possible.

Released: 2021

Studio: Virtual Ravens Games

Plataform: PC

Skill Focus: Production & Game Design

Time Spent: 5 months

Engine and Tools: Phaser, Trello, Jira

Contributions

Producer

My work as production manager focused more on the technical aspects of the game. As it was developed in Phaser, there were quite a few conflicts that caused delays, and we had to manage and coordinate our work as best we could.

Despite this, we were able to polish the game and fix the various bugs that cropped up.

Game Designer

My work within the design department was based on designing the different levels in the game. To do this, tile-based levels were designed to make it easier to control collisions and other aspects.

I also contributed to the design of mechanics to make the game more dynamic and entertaining.
